    Dashboard guide

    How to read this dashboard

    Standings and game scores come from the official Bowl.com U18 Boys reports. Position can change as additional squads finish. There is no official first cut until all bowlers complete 16 qualifying games. Any cut gap or needed-average value marked with an asterisk is a temporary planning estimate, not an official advancement target.

    The year-over-year section compares Jack's final 2025 results with 2026 only at matching four-game checkpoints. The progress chart uses one final official snapshot for each completed 2026 block. “Since your last visit” and favorite bowlers are stored only in the current browser. All schedule and countdown times use Central Time.
